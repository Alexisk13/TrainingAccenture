Rock Music

Rock music is a genre that emerged in the 1950s and became popular worldwide. It is characterized by its strong beats, electric guitar-driven sound, and rebellious attitude.

Rock music incorporates various subgenres such as classic rock, alternative rock, punk rock, and heavy metal. It is known for its energetic performances, passionate lyrics, and often serves as a platform for social and cultural commentary.

Throughout its history, rock music has produced iconic bands and artists, shaping popular culture and influencing subsequent genres. It continues to evolve and diversify, incorporating elements from other musical styles while maintaining its distinctive sound and spirit.
