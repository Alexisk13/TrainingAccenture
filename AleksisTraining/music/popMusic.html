Pop Music

Pop music is a genre that originated in the mid-1950s and gained widespread popularity in the following decades. It is characterized by its catchy melodies, simple song structures, and emphasis on commercial appeal.

Pop music often incorporates elements from various genres, including rock, R&B, electronic, and hip-hop. It is known for its broad appeal and has produced numerous chart-topping hits and iconic artists.

Throughout its history, pop music has evolved and adapted to changing musical trends and technologies. It continues to be a dominant genre in the music industry, influencing popular culture and shaping contemporary music.
